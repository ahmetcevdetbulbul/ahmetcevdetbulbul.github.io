Privacy Policy – ConnectL

Last updated: 24.09.2025

Ludexa Studio (“we”) built the ConnectL game as a free, ad-supported app.

What we collect

We do not ask you to create an account and we do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.

The app stores local game data (e.g., settings, trace color, high scores) only on your device.

The app shows ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and other information required to serve and measure ads.

See Google’s policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Policy

Children

ConnectL is not directed to children under the age of 13.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share personal information. Ad data is processed by Google (AdMob) as described above.

Security

All gameplay data is stored locally on your device. No personal data is stored on our servers.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact us: ludexastudio@gmail.com
